How To Use This

Pick rule: start with Candidate 1, then only override with Candidate 2 on games that appear in the diff table and still feel plausible to you.

Confidence tiers: Locks are stable favorite spots. Leans still favor one side. Toss-ups are real decisions. Volatile games carry wide uncertainty even if one team is favored.

Inspection workflow: use the highlighted Toss-up and Volatile rows as the short list for manual re-checks. Full matchup total-points detail is exported to CSVs if you want a deeper scoring review.

Best pool behavior: late-round volatility matters more than noisy Round of 64 upset hunting under standard scoring.
